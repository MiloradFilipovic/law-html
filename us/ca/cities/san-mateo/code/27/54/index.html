Chapter 27.54 MANUFACTURING DISTRICTS

27.54.010 USE AND BULK REGULATIONS.

27.54.010 USE AND BULK REGULATIONS.

Use and bulk regulations applying specifically to manufacturing districts are set forth in Chapter 27.56. Also applying to manufacturing districts are additional regulations set forth in other chapters of this title as follows:

(1) Chapters 27.02, 27.16, 27.66, 27.68, 27.70 and 27.82, General Provisions;

(2) Chapter 27.04, Definitions;

(3) Chapter 27.72, Nonconforming Buildings and Uses;

(4) Chapter 27.64, Off-street Parking and Loading;

(5) Chapters 27.06 through 27.12, 27.62, 27.74, 27.78 and 27.80, Administration.


(Ord. No. 2009-7 § 28; Prior code § 149.01.)
